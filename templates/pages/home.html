Who We Are

Thank you for visiting the online home of Arbor Heights Bible Church! We are so happy you’ve dropped in and look forward to seeing you in person. But until then, please use this site as a resource. We hope that you will utilize this site to learn more about the Christian faith as well as the ministry of our church to advance the message of the gospel through the building up of the body to the ends of the earth.

We are a Reformed Baptist Church located in Douglasville, GA. From the site menu you can find sermons from our pastors as well as information about who we are and what we believe. At Arbor Heights we believe the Bible to be the final authority for faith and practice. Therefore, we practice the verse by verse exposition of Scripture. Our goal is to glorify God through the faithful preaching and teaching of His word in order that we might equip the saints for the work of the ministry.

We would classify ourselves as an inter-generational congregation, meaning that we encourage families to worship together so that our children learn and participate in the worship service along with their parents. As a convenience, we have a room off the sanctuary with a closed-circuit feed of the worship service if a parent needs to attend to a child’s needs. We hope that you will visit us soon!
